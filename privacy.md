## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

### Introduction

Maker404 ("we," "our," or "us") operates maker404.com and its subdomains (including rotations.maker404.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### Information We Collect

**Automatically Collected Information:**
- We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic and usage patterns
- Basic information like browser type, device type, and pages visited

**Information You Provide:**
- Email addresses if you contact us
- Any information you voluntarily provide through contact forms

**Local Storage:**
- Our rotation planner tool (rotations.maker404.com) stores your roster and schedule data locally in your browser using localStorage
- This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Improve our website and tools
- Respond to inquiries
- Analyze site usage and performance

### Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

### Cookies

Our site uses cookies for:
- Google Analytics (site analytics)
- Google AdSense (advertising)
- Local storage for the rotation planner tool

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Analytics** - Website analytics
- **Google AdSense** - Advertising
- **GitHub Pages** - Website hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information.

### Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

### Children's Privacy

Our site is designed for teachers and coaches (adults). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access information we have about you
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of cookies (via browser settings)

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

### Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at [contact@maker404.com](mailto:contact@maker404.com)
